BRAND | MARKET POSITIONING

Position brand with stakeholders or product in the market
[SaaSify GPS Framework: Values-Strengths-Differentiators]

1

Audience

Target stakeholders for the brand (customers, employees, investors) or product (market / segment).
Analyze stakeholder motivations, preferences, competitive alternatives.

2

Premise

WHAT is the problem? WHY does it need to be solved? WHY now?
Articulate core purpose and promise of the brand or product to the target audience.

3

Proposition

Brand or product value propostion:
WHO - Audience
WHAT – Problem, Product, Value
HOW – Strengths & Differentiators
WHY – Proof Points

4

Messages

Define clear, compelling messages by target audience for top problems and value drivers.
Format messages for consistent action via different channels.

CUSTOMER PROFILE

[SaaSify Funnel Framework: Leads estimate from revenue goal and conversion]

Target Customers

Identify/Select customers and prospects to target.
Segment target customers based on relevant criteria: product-fit, demographic, behavioral or purchase/propensity pattern.

Customer Personas

Develop customer personas: profile (title/role), motivation (pains, priorities, preferences), competitive alternatives (in-house, incumbent, other), constraints (budget, resources, systems).

Contact List

Build and validate contact list for target customers, acquire organically or purchase to fill gaps.
Segment list for campaigns based on customer and persona analysis.

Customer Journey | Content Map

Define customer journey and triggers to reach, engage, nurture (time, event, action etc).
Map content, communications, call-to-action for campaigns.

BRAND | SALES CAMPAIGN

Orchestrate content & communications - digital & offline channels, automation, analytics
[SaaSify Orchestration Framework: Proprietary Tools + Commercial Apps + Crowdsourcing]
Website
Web Pages |  Blog | Content 

Optimize website pages and content for target audience. Add campaign landing page with focus content and call-to-action. Create/Promote blog content and articles relevant to campaign.

Social Media
Conversations | Advertisements

Promote campaign theme and content via social media posts, updates, advertisements (banner, sponsored, etc).
Engage target audience and influencers via conversations relevant to campaign.

Search Engine
Organic SEO | Paid SEM

Analyze keywords/topics and competition. Optimize for search - website, content, links (quality vs quantity). Set budget to buy and place campaign advertisements (AdWords, display, retargeting, etc).

Email & Newsletter
Triggers | Nurture | Call-to-Action

Craft and schedule email communications. Define sequence based on customer journey map. Nurture based on triggers (time, event, action, etc).

VOICE OF CUSTOMER

Capture and incorporate customer voice into strategy, operations, products

Customer Survey

Design/Implement customer survey to capture multi-dimensional feedback: satisfaction, loyalty, advocacy (NPS); experience (key touch-points), brand affinity.
Analyze feedback score to project potential impact and recommend actions.

Interviews / Peer Groups

Design/Conduct 1:1 interviews or facilitate peer group conversations with select customers for in-depth insights.
Analyze inputs to provide actionable guidance to strategy, operations, products.

Advisory Board

Structure Customer Advisory Board for strategic inputs and engagement - charter, composition, cadence, content and more. Setup governance and operations (recruiting, agenda, format, metrics etc).
